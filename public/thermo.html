Process

As a team, we researched competitor shelf products, such as the Nest and Ecobee. With this information, we wanted to enhance our own home system by adding features and design accents they were lacking. Overall we want to take these outdated models, and give customers a new home solution.

We wanted to keep our design simple and clean, like the 3D build of the product. Our tagline for our system is “Comfort at your convenience”.

The Site

We decided to make a single scrolling website as a one-page scrolling site displays a strong design quickly and improves user engagement since users don’t have to click on multiple pages to reach information. We hope this will better engage people to buy our product.
