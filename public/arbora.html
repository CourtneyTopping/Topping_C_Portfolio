Project

The Arbora project was to create a standing and counter display for the products. The different products had to be showcased which included a box as well as a box with a tree already growing out of it. This was challenging, as the boxes could not be stacked as they may be in a normal displays. To maximize the amount of product in our displays, my partner and I decided that that adding shelves would be the most efficient.

The Build

Sticking with the brand theme, we decided on wooden planks as the material to build both displays from. On top, there is a chalkboard-esque display sign with the brand and price. This is used again on the front of the counter display.

The models were rendered out in Cinema4d with the sign being made in Photoshop. Along with the mock-ups, we also did a full spec sheet, detailing the sizes of all the pieces involved in the display.
